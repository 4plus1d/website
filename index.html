Introduction

Hello, I'm Ram Prakash. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Physics from IIT Bombay and am currently a Ph.D. student at the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS-TIFR), Bangalore.

My interests lies in the intersection of Physics and Mathematics, particularly in Quantum Field Theory, Gravity and Geometry.

Through this blog, I share notes, thoughts, and expository pieces on topics where mathematics and physics meet, aiming to present ideas clearly and thoughtfully, without losing their underlying depth.
